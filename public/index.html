How SoundGrid Works!

SongGrid is a data visualization tool for songs, turning the audio experience of music into a visual canvas. Song data is obtained from the Spotify API, then analyzed and transposed to a color-coded grid.

Each note from a song is classified by it's correspondence to one of twelve possible notes -- A, A#/Bb, B, C, C#/Db, D, D#/Eb, E, F, F#/Gb, G, G#/Ab -- and mapped to a unique color. Experiment with different songs and color mappings!
